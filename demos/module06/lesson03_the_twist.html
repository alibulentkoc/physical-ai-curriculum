Set the twist (v_O, ω). Every point's velocity is v = v_O + ω×r. The whole field is one rigid motion, spinning about the instantaneous centre — the single point momentarily at rest.
The twist bundles linear and angular velocity into one object. The instantaneous centre sits at (−v_Oy/ω, v_Ox/ω); when ω = 0 the motion is pure translation and the centre flies to infinity.
