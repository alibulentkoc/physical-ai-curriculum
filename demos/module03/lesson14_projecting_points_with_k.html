Move the 3D point (X, Y, Z) and fₓ. Each point projects through K to one pixel — but only if it passes two checks: in front (Z > 0) and in frame.
Projection is per-point: u = fₓ·X/Z + cₓ. Push Z behind the camera (negative) and the point is invalid; lower fₓ and an off-centre point can fall back inside the frame.
