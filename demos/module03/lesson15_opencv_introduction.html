Slide the camera along its own axis (tvec z) and toggle distortion. Watch Z_c and the output pixel change — and see OpenCV match the hand-built pipeline when distortion is zero.
cv2.projectPoints runs object → [R|t] → K → pixel. Moving the camera forward shrinks Z_c, so the pixel pushes outward. With distCoeffs = 0 it equals our formula exactly; nonzero coeffs bend it.
