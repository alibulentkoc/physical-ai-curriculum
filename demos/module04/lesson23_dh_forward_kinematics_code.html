Evaluating the DH table is forward kinematics. Spin θ₁ and the whole reach rotates about the vertical axis; the in-plane shape (from θ₂, θ₃) is exactly the 2-link arm and doesn't change with θ₁.
The in-plane reach √(x²+y²+z²) depends only on θ₂ and θ₃ — the base swivel just chooses the compass direction. That's why a 3-DOF arm's vertical-plane behaviour reduces to the 2-link case.
