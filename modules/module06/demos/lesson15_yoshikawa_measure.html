Sweep θ₂ and read w = √det(JJᵀ) = σ₁σ₂. One number for dexterity: the ellipse's area. For a planar 2R arm it peaks at θ₂ = ±90° and vanishes when the arm is straight (θ₂ = 0 or 180°).
w = L₁L₂|sin θ₂| here. Path planners maximise w to keep the arm away from singularities, trading a longer route for steady, controllable motion.
