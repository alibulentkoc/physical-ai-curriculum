SVD Bars + Condition Number

The bars are the singular values σ₁, σ₂ — the ellipse's axis lengths. As the arm nears a singularity the σ₂ bar collapses, the ellipse flattens, and the condition number κ = σ₁/σ₂ climbs. The decomposition is the geometry: J·vi = σi·ui.
