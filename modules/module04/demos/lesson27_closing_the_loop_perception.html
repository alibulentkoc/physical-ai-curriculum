Grab the fruit and move it; drive the arm. The same fruit, in three frames: camera → base (add the mount) → gripper (apply the inverse of T₀ⁿ). The gripper-frame vector is the grasp command.
A pure-translation mount means P_base = P_cam + mount. Then (T₀ⁿ)⁻¹ re-expresses that base point in the gripper's own frame — telling the controller exactly which way, and how far, to close in.
