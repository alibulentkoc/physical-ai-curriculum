Click the handle to grab it, move, then click again to drop (or just move off the canvas). Then you can use the slider.
Try it: drag the teal body to set its position, and use the slider to turn it — together they make the pose (x, y, θ).
A full pose = where (drag the body) + which way it faces (slider). Both numbers together are what one transformation will carry — the idea the rest of Module 2 makes precise.
