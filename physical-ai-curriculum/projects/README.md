# `projects/` — Projects & Capstone

Hands-on **project scaffolding** that ties the curriculum together — most importantly the running **Greenhouse Harvesting Robot** narrative and the final Digital Twin capstone.

## Purpose

Where `modules/` teaches concepts topic by topic, `projects/` is where students *integrate* them into a working system. Every module contributes a piece; the projects assemble those pieces into a perception-to-action pipeline.

## The core narrative

All modules revolve around one system: a **Greenhouse Harvesting Robot**. Across the curriculum, students progressively build the models needed to:

- perceive fruit
- estimate position
- transform coordinate frames
- compute kinematics
- plan motion
- communicate commands
- actuate motors
- construct a digital twin
