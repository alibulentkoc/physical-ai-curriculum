# Answer Key 8.4 — Unit 8 Recap, Module 9 Close, and the Handoff to Module 10

*Coaches' key. Multiple choice followed by model short answers and grading notes.*

---

**Q1 — B.** One self-healing harvester — a six-stage pick cycle looped across a row.

**Q2 — B.** Wrap don't redefine, coordinate don't re-theorise, make seams explicit.

**Q3 — B.** harvest_row — perception to recovery to row orchestration, in one call.

**Q4 — B.** Module 10 mirrors the system for simulation, visualisation, and validation (single robot).

**Q5 — True.** No new estimation, planning, or control theory — the intelligence is in the composition.

---

**Q6 — model answer.** Units 1–2 (Perceive → Understand): turn raw detections into a committed, deduplicated target. Units 3–4 (Plan, Execute): turn that target into a validated reference and drive it into tracked motion. Units 5–6 (Track, Detect): judge the motion and detect-and-localise any failure from existing signals. Units 7–8 (Recover, Integrate): respond to faults by targeted bounded recovery and loop the cycle across a row with graceful degradation. Eight units, four contributions, one self-healing system.
*Grading: credit a correct one-line contribution for each of the four unit pairs.*
