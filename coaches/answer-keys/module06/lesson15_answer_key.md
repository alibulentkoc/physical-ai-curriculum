# Answer Key 4.3
**Q1 B · Q2 B · Q3 B · Q4 B · Q5 B.**
**Q6.** $w=\sqrt{\det(JJ^\top)}=\prod_i\sigma_i$ = the ellipsoid's volume.
**Q7.** $w$ measures volume, not shape; a thin and a round ellipsoid of equal area share $w$ but differ greatly in usability — the condition number (L6.3) captures the difference.
**Q8.** $w=|L_1L_2\sin\theta_2|$, maximized at $\theta_2=90^\circ$ (and $-90^\circ$). 
### Watch for: treating $w$ as the whole story; forgetting the $JJ^\top$ form for redundant arms.
