**Q6.** $\tau^\top\dot q=F^\top\xi=F^\top J\dot q$ for all $\dot q\Rightarrow \tau=J^\top F$.
**Q7.** A direction the tool moves easily (long velocity axis) is one it can push only weakly (short force axis), and vice versa.
