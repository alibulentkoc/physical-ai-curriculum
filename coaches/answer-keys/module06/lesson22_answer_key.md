# Answer Key 6.2
**Q1 B · Q2 B · Q3 B · Q4 B · Q5 B.**
**Q6.** κ = σ_max/σ_min ≥ 1; κ=1 is perfectly isotropic (a round ellipsoid / sphere), larger κ is more elongated.
**Q7.** w (volume) is blind to shape — two poses can share w with different shapes; κ (axis-length ratio) distinguishes them (e.g. θ₂=0.6 vs π−0.6: same w, κ≈8.1 vs 1.9).
**Q8.** κ rises toward ∞ before σ_min actually reaches zero, giving advance warning to reroute or damp.
### Watch for: using w alone; reading small κ as large capability (κ is shape, not size).
