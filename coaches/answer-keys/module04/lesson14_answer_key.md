# Answer Key — Lesson 4.2: Position and Orientation of the Gripper

**Coaches only.** Formative.

1. **Position** — the translation column of T_0^n.
2. **Rotation block columns** — the gripper's x, y, z axes expressed in the base frame.
3. **Grasping needs both position and orientation** — True.
4. **Approach direction (short).** A column of the rotation block (e.g. the gripper's z-axis ẑ_g) gives the approach direction.

**Challenge rubric.** Full credit: a 6-DOF arm can usually hold position while rotating the gripper (extra orientation freedom), letting it approach fruit from a clear angle among obstacles. Partial: states position/orientation can vary independently without the obstacle-avoidance motivation.
