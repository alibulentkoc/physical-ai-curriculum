# Answer Key — Lesson 4.1: The General Chain T₀ⁿ(θ)

**Coaches only.** Formative.

1. **General FK map** — T_0^n(θ) = ∏_{i=1}^n T_{i-1}^i(θi).
2. **Each factor** — an SE(3) element depending on one joint variable.
3. **Order matters (rotations don't commute)** — True.
4. **Why FK is easy to evaluate (short).** It's a product of known matrices — one pass, always defined, one answer (unlike the inverse, which solves an equation).

**Challenge rubric.** Full credit: frames forward as "evaluate a function" (always defined, single answer, cheap) vs inverse as "solve an equation" (nonlinear, 0/1/many solutions, often no closed form). Partial: states forward easy / inverse hard without the function-vs-equation framing.
