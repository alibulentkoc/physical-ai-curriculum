# Answer Key — Lesson 2.1: A One-Joint Arm

**Coaches only.** Formative.

1. **Gripper position** — (L cos θ, L sin θ).
2. **Path as θ varies** — a circle of radius L.
3. **Orientation = θ** — True.
4. **L=0.5, θ=45° (short).** (0.354, 0.354).

**Challenge rubric.** Full credit: with a fixed 90° tip bend the orientation block gains a constant +90° offset, but the position stays (L cos θ, L sin θ) because the tip's *location* is unchanged by a re-orientation of the gripper about that point. Partial: correct position invariance without the orientation offset.
