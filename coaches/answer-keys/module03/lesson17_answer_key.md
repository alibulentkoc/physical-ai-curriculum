# Answer Key — Lesson 5.1: Why Straight Lines Bend

**Coaches only.** Formative; unlimited attempts.

1. **Lens distortion is** — a deviation of a real lens from the ideal pinhole model.
2. **Distortion is largest** — near the edges (grows with radius).
3. **Barrel outward / pincushion inward** — True.
4. **Edge mislocalization (short).** Distortion grows with radius, so detections near the edge are displaced from the ideal pinhole location and mislocalized; the center is nearly distortion-free.

**Challenge rubric.** Full credit: identifies distortion as the radius-dependent cause of edge-only error, and proposes undistortion (or modeling distCoeffs) in the perception pipeline. Partial: notes edge error without the fix.
