# Physical AI Curriculum

Welcome. This is the student-facing site for a curriculum in **Physical AI, Robotics, Computer Vision, Mechatronics, and Digital Twins** — built around a single running system, a **Greenhouse Harvesting Robot**.

Every topic is taught in five layers: physical intuition → visual understanding → mathematical formulation → computational implementation → system integration. You'll understand not just *how* the equations work, but *why* they matter in a real robot.

## Start here

- [**Lesson 1.1 — Physical AI and the Physical World**](module01/lesson01.md) — the orientation lesson and the prototype of the full student experience (reading, diagram, notebook, interactive demo, and knowledge check).

## How the materials fit together

Each lesson comes with:

- a **reading** (this site),
- a runnable **notebook** for the hands-on parts,
- **visuals** that build intuition,
- an **interactive demo**, and
- a formative **knowledge check** (unlimited attempts, immediate feedback — it doesn't affect your grade).
