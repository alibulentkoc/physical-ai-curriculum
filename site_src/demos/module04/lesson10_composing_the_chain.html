Drive both joints. The chain T₀² = T₀¹(θ₁)·T₁²(θ₂) is one transform. Its rotation block is θ₁+θ₂ — the joint angles simply add.
Multiplying the per-joint transforms composes them: the rotations add and the translation column lands the gripper. That single product is the forward kinematics of the two-link arm.
