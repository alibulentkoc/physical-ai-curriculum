Set the geometry between two joint axes. Two of the four DH numbers come straight from it: the twist α (angle between axes) and the offset a (common-normal length). Try the presets.
Parallel axes have no twist between them, so α = 0. Intersecting axes meet at a point, so the common normal has zero length, a = 0. The other two numbers, θ and d, slide and spin along the shared axis.
