Swivel and bend the 3-DOF arm. The base joint turns the whole arm about the vertical axis — that's the row carrying the 90° twist (α). The two in-plane links carry the link lengths in a.

The 90° twist on row 1 is what tips the horizontal base axis up into the arm's working plane. Rows 2–3 are planar links, so their α is 0 and their a holds the lengths 0.3 and 0.2.
