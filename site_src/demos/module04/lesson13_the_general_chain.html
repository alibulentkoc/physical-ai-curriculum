Drive the three joints. T₀³ = T₀¹·T₁²·T₂³ — one factor per joint. Turn on the reordered chain (dashed) to see that multiplying the same factors in a different order lands somewhere else.
An n-joint arm has n factors. Changing the last joint still moves the tip; and because matrix multiplication doesn't commute, reordering the factors generally changes the result.
