Slide the distortion k₁. Negative = barrel (lines bow outward), positive = pincushion (lines bow inward). The centre barely moves; the edges bend most.
Radial distortion scales each point by (1 + k₁·r²), where r is the distance from the centre. Because of the r², the correction is tiny near the middle and grows fast toward the edges — that's why straight lines bow only at the rim.
