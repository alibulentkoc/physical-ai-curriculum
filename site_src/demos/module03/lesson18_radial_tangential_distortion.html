Click the point to grab it, move, click to drop. Slide k₁ too. The distorted point (red) = (1 + k₁·r²)·(ideal). Drag toward the edge and the shift grows fast; at the centre it's zero.
The correction scales with r², so doubling the distance from the centre roughly quadruples the shift. With k₁ = 0 the point doesn't move at all — distortion is purely radial here.
