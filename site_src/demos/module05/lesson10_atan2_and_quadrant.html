Grab the target — drag it behind the base (x < 0). atan2(y, x) (green) points correctly; arctan(y/x) (dashed red) loses the quadrant and aims the opposite way.
arctan only returns angles in (−90°, 90°), so it can't tell left from right. atan2 uses the signs of both x and y to land in the correct one of four quadrants — essential for shoulder angles.
