Grab the target and walk it outward. With L₁=L₂=0.3 there are two solutions (elbow-up & elbow-down) inside, exactly one at r=0.6 (arm straight), and zero beyond. Watch the two poses merge at the edge.
Inverse kinematics is nonlinear, so a target can have 0, 1, or 2 solutions. The two interior solutions coincide right at the workspace boundary, where the arm is fully stretched.
