Grab the target; sweep the wrist orientation φ. A 3-link arm has a spare degree of freedom — infinitely many whole-arm poses reach the same point. That free choice is the redundancy a numerical solver must resolve.
Fixing φ turns the spare freedom into a plain 2-link sub-problem for the first joints. With no single answer, a solver needs a rule — shortest move, joint-limit-safe — to pick one.
