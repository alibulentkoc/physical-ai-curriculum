Grab the target. Base, elbow, and target form a triangle with sides L₁, L₂, and r. The law of cosines gives the bend: cos θ₂ = (r² − L₁² − L₂²) / (2 L₁ L₂).
As the target nears full reach (r → 0.7) the triangle flattens and cos θ₂ → 1 (straight). Pull it close (r → 0.1) and it folds, cos θ₂ → −1. At r = 0.5 the elbow is a right angle.
