Click the handle to grab it, move, then click again to drop (or just move off the canvas). Then you can use the slider.
Try it: drag the object to set its position, use the slider to rotate it, then tick the box to drop the rotation and see what a bare point loses.
A pose packs position (translation column) and orientation (rotation block) into one matrix. Drop the rotation and you have a bare point — the gripper would know where to go but not how to face, so the grasp is undefined.
