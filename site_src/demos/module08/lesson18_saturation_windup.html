Tighten the actuator limit. When the command clips at u_max, the integral keeps accumulating error it can't act on — winding up. When the joint finally catches up, that stored excess causes a big overshoot. Anti-windup stops the accumulation.
Saturation is unavoidable — every motor has a ceiling. The fix isn't more power, it's stopping the integral from charging up while the command is pinned at the limit.
